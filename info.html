Our Planet

Blooming Responsibly

At Fleur & Co, beauty and responsibility are inseparable. Every decision we make — from the farms we partner with to the ribbon we wrap your bouquet in — is guided by our commitment to a greener world.

🌱

Ethical Sourcing

We work exclusively with Rainforest Alliance–certified farms that prioritise fair wages, safe conditions, and biodiversity.

♻️

Zero-Waste Packaging

All our packaging is 100% compostable or recycled. Our signature ribbon is made from organic cotton — no single-use plastic, ever.

🚗

Carbon-Neutral Delivery

We offset 100% of delivery emissions through verified reforestation projects. Our local routes use electric vehicles.

🌍

Our 2025 Pledge

By the end of 2025, Fleur & Co will be fully carbon-neutral across our entire supply chain — from seed to doorstep. We plant one tree for every order placed.

Read Our Full Sustainability Report

Local Sourcing Wherever Possible

Wherever seasonally possible we source from UK and European growers, drastically reducing air freight miles. Our seasonal edits — Spring, Summer, Autumn, and Winter — are designed entirely around what is naturally flourishing, not what can be forced.

Community Impact

5% of all profits go directly to our Bloom Forward fund, which supports urban greening projects, school garden programmes, and mental health initiatives that use floristry as therapy across the UK.

Flower Care

Make Your Blooms Last

With just a little love and attention, your Fleur & Co arrangement can look stunning for up to two weeks. Here is everything you need to know.

✂️

Trim the Stems

As soon as your flowers arrive, cut 2–3cm from each stem at a 45° angle. This creates a larger surface area for water absorption. Repeat every 2–3 days.

💧

Fresh Water Daily

Change your vase water every day, or every other day at minimum. Use lukewarm — not cold — water. Add the included flower food sachet on day one.

🌡️

Cool Placement

Keep flowers away from direct sunlight, radiators, and fruit bowls (fruit releases ethylene gas which accelerates wilting). A cool, bright spot is ideal.

🍃

Remove Leaves Below Water

Strip any leaves that fall below the waterline — submerged foliage rots quickly and introduces bacteria that shortens your flowers' lifespan significantly.

🌙

Night-Time Cool-Down

On warm evenings, move your arrangement to a cooler room overnight — even a bathroom or kitchen works well. Cooler temperatures dramatically extend vase life.

🪣

Keep the Vase Clean

Rinse your vase with a small amount of white vinegar once a week to prevent bacteria build-up. A clean vase can add 2–3 extra days to your arrangement's life.

💐

Flower-Specific Care

Different blooms have different needs. Roses love cool water and a single aspirin. Tulips continue to grow in the vase — trim every 2 days. Peonies open fastest in warm rooms. Sunflowers are thirsty — top up water daily.

Ask Our Florists Directly

When to Arrange vs. Leave in Paper

If your flowers arrive tightly budded, leave them in the paper wrapping with stems in a tall glass of water for 4–6 hours. This allows them to open naturally before you arrange them in a vase. Rushing this process can shock the stems.

Dried & Preserved Flowers

For any dried or preserved arrangements in your order, simply keep them away from humidity and direct sunlight. Dust gently with a soft brush once a month. With minimal care, preserved arrangements can last 12–24 months.
