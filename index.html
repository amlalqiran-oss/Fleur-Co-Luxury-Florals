Our Story

Born from a Passion
for Beautiful Things

Fleur & Co was born in a small Paris-inspired studio in 2018. Our founder Sophie believed flowers deserved more than just a vase — they deserved to be art. Today, we source the world's most beautiful blooms and compose them into arrangements that stir the soul.
